Birth

JAHCTIVATION was created at the beginning of the years 2000 in Belgium to promote positive vibrations and develop the sound system tradition. Jahctivation are original roaring vibes from Belgium/Northern France,playing and producing Roots/Dub/Steppers, Ready to play all over whenever you're ready !!!

Youth

In 2008 the Haile I Dub's dances, 1st regular dub sessions runned in Brussels (Relais TIR). The Haile I Dub came later in Lille-FR (2012). For the all seasons of HID, we've been inviting and promoting the local sound system's and high level producers, soundmen and voices as Kibir La Amlak, Stamina Li, Claire Ray, David Judah, Terry Gad, Roots Hitek, Bobo Black Star, Dougie Conscious, Coptic Sound, Rootikal Warriah, King Alpha, Ion Youth, Revelation Hifi, Jah Militant, Jah Voice, Agobun, Kenny Knots, I Bingi Sound, Kheru Jahman Dan, Unique Sounds, Murray Man, Ras Muffet ...

Every time the Jahctivation family grew up bigger, creating real friendships through the time between the soundactivists.
